Studies
I am a MSc student at Saarland University, in the Language Science and Technology Master's program. I finished my undergraduate studies in the University of Patras, with a B.A. in Greek Philology, Major in Linguistics.

Work
I work at the Deutsches Forschungszentrum für Kunstliche Intelligenz (DFKI), in the Multilingual Techologies lab, under Prof. Dr. Günter Neumann. Our current project is called iRead, an EU H2020 project, in which we contribute to the creation of new technologies for multilingual text classification, syntactic parsing, dictionary generation and automatic linguistic annotation.
