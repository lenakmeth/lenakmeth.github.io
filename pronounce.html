Studies
I am a grad student at Saarland University, in the [Language Science and Technology]() Master's program. I finished my undergraduate studies in the University of Patras, with a B.A. in Greek Philology, Major in Linguistics.

Work
I work at the [Deutsches Forschungszentrum für Kunstliche Intelligenz](dfki.de) (DFKI), in the Multilingual Techologies lab, under Prof. Dr. [Günter Neumann](http://dfki.de/~neumann/). Our current project is called [iRead](https://iread-project.eu/), an EU H2020 project, in which we contribute to the creation of new technologies for multilingual text classification, syntactic parsing, dictionary generation and automatic linguistic annotation.
